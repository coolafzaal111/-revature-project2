Our Services

Helpers app is a platform that helps customers book reliable and high quality services- appliance repair, painting, handymen, tutor and more - delivered by trained professionals conviniently at your location. Helpers' vision is to empower professionals to deliver services at home like never experienced before.

Customers can login to their account and avail various services like- Electrician, Plumber, Builder, Carpenter, Tutor, Mechanic and others- at their location just by selecting the service they need and finding the deatils of the helpers. Also, the persons who are trained can register themselves to provide the services and be helper.

Welcome to the Admin Dashboard

This is the Admin portal dashboard of Helpers App. On this page various options are provided on the left nav bar regarding Customers, Helpers, Our Services and Admin. The details of customers and helpers can be accesed by selecting the particular option and admin can alter as well as delete the details of customers and helpers. Our services option reveals the information about services that is provided by us. Details of Admin can be accessed by clicking the Admin option.
